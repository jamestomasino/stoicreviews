Stoicism teaches that virtue
is based on knowledge.

The wise live in harmony with reason,

and are indifferent to the whims of fortune and to pleasure and pain.

Philosophy meets real life

We think about how to be the best version of ourselves. What actions can we take, how to we spend our energy, what guides our thoughts? Here we focus on how these questions play out in characters or scenes from entertainment, media, daily life and history. Where do we find stoic choices, where would it have helped?
